Set for a 2014 release date, Jurassic Park 4 will be a continuation of the previous Jurassic Park Trilogy (Jurassic Park, Jurassic Park 2, Jurassic Park 3). Jurassic Park 4 will not be directed by Steven Spielberg, but Spielberg will have an influence on the film's production.

Jurassic Park 4

Jurassic Park 4 is the fourth installment in the legendary Jurassic Park trilogy - kicking off an entirely new storyline and starting a brand new Jurassic Park trilogy. In itself, Jurassic Park 4 has no official release date or title yet, but will be produced by Jurassic Park Director, Steven Spielberg and directed by Joe Johnston who directed the third Jurassic Park film, Jurassic Park 3. Cast and Plot informaiton for Jurassic Park 4 have not yet been officially released. Watch JurassicPark4-Movie.com for the latest news and information on Jurassic Park 4.
